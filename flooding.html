State name, county name, number of properties , and population are displayed when hovering over each county. Counties with the lightest color blue have missing Flood Factor data.

The risk value (Flood Factor) was developed by First Street Foundation which is a non-profit dedicated to quantifying and communicating risks to inform Americans of their risks from environmental changes.

Flood Factor determine the risk of being impacted by flooding by considering:

  • high intensity rainfall
  • overflowing rivers and streams
  • high tides
  • coastal storm surge
